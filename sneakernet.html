[BRIEF-01]
WHAT IS A PACKET
A portable JSON file containing your identity, active projects, and AI conversation history. Upload it at session start — any AI picks up exactly where you left off.
[BRIEF-02]
WHY SNEAKERNET
AI platforms don't share memory. SneakerNet bridges them — no cloud, no subscription, no vendor lock-in. Your context lives on your device and travels with you.
[BRIEF-03]
